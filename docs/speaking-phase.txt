🧠 AI Speaking Evaluation

Sau khi có transcript:

text + audio -> evaluate
Evaluation Criteria

Theo IELTS rubric:

1️⃣ Fluency and coherence

Check:

speech pauses

filler words

sentence flow

2️⃣ Lexical resource

Check:

vocabulary variety

idioms

repetition

3️⃣ Grammar

Check:

sentence complexity

tense accuracy

4️⃣ Pronunciation

Check:

phoneme accuracy

stress patterns
